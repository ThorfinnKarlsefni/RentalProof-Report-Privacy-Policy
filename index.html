Support

For support, bug reports, or privacy questions, contact the developer through the support email listed on the App Store product page.

When reporting an issue, include your iOS version, device model, and what you were doing before the issue happened. Do not send private lease documents unless requested.

RentalProof Report does not provide legal advice. It helps create a personal record of rental condition.
