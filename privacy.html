Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

RentalProof Report is a local-first iOS app for creating rental inspection notes and reports.

Data Collection

RentalProof Report does not collect, sell, rent, or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

Local Data

The app stores inspection details, notes, and user-selected photos locally on the user's device. This data is used only to display the inspection and generate a PDF report.

Sharing

If the user chooses to share a generated PDF report, the report is shared through Apple's standard iOS sharing interface. The destination is chosen by the user.

Tracking

RentalProof Report does not track users across apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

RentalProof Report does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote storage services.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the App Store product page.
